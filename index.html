    Hello,

    I'm Verity Stothard, a Front End Web Developer based in Sydney, Australia.

    With a background in small business management and marketing, I’m obsessed with how things work. I have a deep rooted passion for working with entrepreneurs and creative types to drive there vision forward on the web.

    After earning my Bachelor of Business from the University of Technology, Sydney, I took a year off to try my hand at entrepreneurship and worked out very quickly that to be successful in business, you need to be successful online, and that’s where I found my true passion.

    These days. I live and breathe Web Development (often well into the small hours of the morning), but you won’t get any complex tech jargon here. I believe in making the tech world as accessible as possible, because who knows, your idea might just be the one that changes the world.
